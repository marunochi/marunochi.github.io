Project Overview

Atticus Conseil is a dual-business model venture combining wealth advisory services for Wealth Managers with B2B digital transformation solutions for private banks and family offices.

The flagship product, Atticus Chat, is a scalable SaaS platform with AI integration designed specifically for the French financial markets, targeting CGP advisors, private banking, and HNWI clients.

The Challenge

Problem: French wealth management advisors face increasing regulatory complexity, time-consuming client reporting, and lack of specialized AI tools adapted to French financial markets and compliance requirements.

Solution: Atticus Chat provides automated financial analysis, regulatory compliance tools, and intelligent client reporting systems specifically designed for the French wealth management ecosystem, enabling advisors to focus on client relationships while maintaining regulatory excellence.
