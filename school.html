About Dauphine-PSL

Université Paris-Dauphine is a leading public research university specialized in organization and decision sciences. Member of PSL University (Paris Sciences et Lettres), ranked among the top French universities.

The Finance program at Dauphine is recognized as one of the best in Europe, with strong connections to the financial industry and a curriculum combining theoretical rigor with practical applications.
